Matt's Technical Notes:

  • The small and big blind are to the left of the player with the dealer button in order, and the button shifts clockwise before each hand.
  • Before the flop, players take turns in clockwise fashion starting with the player to the left of the small blind. During subsequent betting rounds, actions starts with the player to the left of the button and the dealer is last to act.
  • There needs to be a section in the middle of the table for the community cards and pot. In front of each player, a little stack of chips and their hole cards if they have not folded.
  • A 30 second timer for each player's turn. After the time runs out the player's hand is folded.
  • The card shuffling algorithm needs to be sufficient to ensure game fairness.
  • The logic to determine hand strengths and winning hand(s) is likely to be the most intricate part of the implementation.
